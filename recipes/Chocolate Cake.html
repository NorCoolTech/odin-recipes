Chocolate Cake

Home

Description

This classic chocolate cake is moist, fluffy, and full of deep chocolate flavor. Topped with a smooth chocolate frosting, it’s a dessert that never disappoints. Perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or simply to satisfy your sweet tooth.

It’s simple to make and tastes even better the next day!

Ingredients

  • 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, mix eggs, milk, oil, sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients, then slowly add boiling water (batter will be thin).
  5. Pour the batter into pans and bake for 30–35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool for 10 minutes, then remove from pans and cool completely before frosting.
  7. Frost with your favorite chocolate icing and enjoy!
