Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a comforting Italian favorite made with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It’s perfect for family dinners, gatherings, or whenever you’re craving something hearty and delicious.

Although it takes a bit of time to prepare, the result is absolutely worth it — a golden, bubbling dish that brings everyone to the table.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 800g canned tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 200g mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 100g parmesan cheese, grated
  • 400ml béchamel or white sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pan, heat olive oil and sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  4. Add the ground meat and cook until browned. Stir in tomato paste, tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, layer the ingredients: start with a thin layer of sauce, then noodles, béchamel, cheese, and repeat until all ingredients are used.
  6. Top with mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake for about 30–40 minutes, until the top is golden and bubbling.
  8. Let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
